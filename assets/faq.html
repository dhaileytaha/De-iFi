DeFi is Decentralized Finance. Where we can do all finance activities without a traditional bank using cryptocurrencies. It might invovle few corporations sometime.

Many DeFi applicaitons have elements that does not fit in Islamic Finance, so with DeiFi we like to create a guide for individuals who are looking for green and ethical DeFi. In future, we also like to create potential DeFi applicaitons that can explicitly support Islamic Finance.

Islamic Finance is prevailent only in few regions suchs as South East Asia and the Middle East
Hence Islamic Finance have poor connectivity and also highly censored. There are many people who cannot open an account in an Islamic Bank because of identity reasons.
There are Muslims and non-muslims who would like to invest in green and ethical markets. So DeiFi is to help solve the problem of connectivity and censorship for Islamic Finance
